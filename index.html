Trivia

  1. Beagles have an excellent sense of smell.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting hares.
  3. They are one of the most popular dog breeds in the world.
  4. Beagles are known for their friendly and curious nature.
  5. Their barking can be quite loud and melodious.
